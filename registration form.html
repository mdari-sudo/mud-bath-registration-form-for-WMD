Mangrove Mud Bath Marathon 2023

This registration form is for those willing to participate in the mangrove mud bath marathon. The main objective is to raise awareness on the importance of conserving the mangrove ecosystem.

Mombasa County has witnessed loss of mangroves forests through encroachment, shoreline erosion, pollution leading to reduce fish stocks, increased temperatures rendering the forest adjacent communities vulnerable to effects of climate change.

This mangrove mud bath marathon has been set up to raise funds so as to achieve 3 objectives:

  1. To set up beacons to highlight the shoreline of the forest
  2. To restructure the community forest association who work together with the Kenya forest service as required by FCMA 2016 in the protection, conservation and management of the forest
  3. To implement the National Mangrove Ecosystem Management Plan

The marathon has been scheduled for 22nd July 2023 starting at 8 am in the morning at Makupa Roundabout to Mikindani Mangrove area. For you to be part of this conservation effort, register below.

Registration is open to individuals (2 types) or as groups of 5 members in an organization or institutions. You can register as many groups as you wish for each category in each institution or organization. There are 3 categories: 7 Km, 5 Km, and 2 Km.

Welcome to this FUN GAME as you learn more about the importance of the conservation of the mangrove ecosystem.
